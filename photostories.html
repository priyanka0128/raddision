WEDDING PHOTO STORIES

The wedding is the most important day in an individual’s life. Every couple wants to take their beautiful love stories to the next level – wedding. Hence, they leave no stone unturned to make the do a perfect one. Everything, from trousseau to menu to the venue are meticulously planned and executed. However, when it comes to documenting a wedding story, a lot of couples still leave the job to a friend or an amateur photographer. Although there is no harm in doing so, we can guarantee that amateur eyes can never be as good as a professional one in portraying the romantic stories of couples. A good photographer will never miss a moment and will weave an amazing wedding short story you can cherish forever. Moreover, they can also suggest a few wedding love story ideas.

In India, the wedding day is a busy one with innumerable rituals. Each function signifies the eternal bond that a couple shares. There are a plethora of emotions that rule each wedding. Hence, it is important to hire a professional, experienced in wedding story photography; a photographer who understands the rituals and can capture the moments rather than shallow poses. The genuine smiles, the subtle eye contacts between the couple and the tears – all call for a story about love. Documenting a photo love story is even more important because human memories are short-lived and there is no chance for a recap of the fleeting moments; neither can they be recreated.

If the idea of a wedding love story is giving you jitters, we would suggest you go for a pre wedding story or an engagement love story. This will eliminate camera-shyness and will better prepare you for your wedding ceremony love story. After all, modern photography is all about candid photography and you can’t really look stiff on your marriage love stories. Most of the professional photographers offer pre wedding love story shoots as a part of the entire package. They can help you out with beautiful pre wedding photoshoot ideas. One of the most popular ones is how we met stories, which you can display at the venue.

For a photographer too, when it comes to documenting wedding stories India is the best place to be. The multitude of colorful rituals and the myriad emotions offer a perfect backdrop for wedding stories, how we met tales and gorgeous receptions alike. Our love story, wedding snaps etc. pass to our future generation as an heirloom. It can be seen as an example of short love story, reinstating one’s faith in the institution of love.

So if you are getting married soon, be sure to hire a professional for your couple stories. Browse through our featured wedding photo stories for some inspiration; you are most likely to fall in love with these instantly.
